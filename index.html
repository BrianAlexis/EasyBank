EasyBank is an app and a free international Mastercard prepaid card, so you can manage and save your money easier. With the Easybank card you can buy in any store and website in the world that accepts Mastercard. In addition, you can send and receive money to / from other Easybank users, bank accounts (CBU) and virtual accounts (CVU) for free and instantly. You can also recharge prepaid telephony and television services.

You must have a valid ID and be over 13 years old. If you are between 13 and 17 years old, you can have your Ualá with the authorization of your father / mother or legal guardian.

You can load your EasyBank with cash and by bank and / or virtual transfer (Home Banking and app). If you want to do it from a bank and / or virtual account, look for your CVU / Alias in the "Upload" section of the Ualá app. Share your CVU with whoever you want or enter the transfers section of your bank or virtual account, complete the amount with your CVU / Alias and confirm the transfer. CVU transfers are credited on the spot and are also totally FREE. Remember that ATMs still do not allow transferring to a CVU.
